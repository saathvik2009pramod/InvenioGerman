Visit German Cities

Explore highlights and quick facts.

Berlin

Capital city known for history, culture, and nightlife.

  • Brandenburg Gate
  • Museum Island
  • Vibrant startup scene

Munich

Bavarian capital famous for engineering, beer gardens, and Oktoberfest.

  • Marienplatz & Glockenspiel
  • BMW Welt & museums
  • Nearby Alps

Hamburg

Major port city with strong maritime history and modern architecture.

  • Elbphilharmonie concert hall
  • Historic Speicherstadt
  • Harbor tours

Frankfurt

Financial hub with a striking skyline and strong commerce links.

  • European Central Bank
  • Römer & historic old town
  • International trade fairs

Tech in Germany

Germany is a global leader in automotive engineering, industrial machinery, and increasingly in AI research.

Automotive

Home to iconic brands like BMW, Mercedes-Benz, Audi, and Volkswagen — strong on EV and engineering innovation.

Engineering

Robust mechanical and industrial engineering traditions with precision manufacturing and large-scale R&D.

AI & Research

Growing AI hubs in Berlin, Munich, and elsewhere with close industry-academia collaboration.
